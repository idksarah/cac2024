Colorblindness IRL

Accessible user interface designs are crucial for ensuring that all users, including those with colorblindness, are able to enjoy the internet and interact with digital content with ease. Designs that incorporate colorblind awareness into their thinking are likely to be more successful, as well as better the user experience of all involved. We hope that you can take the lessons learned from these mini games into your future web development, product management, and the rest of your life. Thank you!
